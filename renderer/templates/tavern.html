The Tavern

The Tavern is Borderland Keep's Mastodon instance. Mastodon is a federated version of microblogging platforms like twitter or threads. Sign up just like you would on any of those platforms. Once signed up You will be able to access other mastodon instances. No need to make multiple accounts for Borderland Keep, Dice Camp or any other instance. You cross post and interact with them to your heart's content.
